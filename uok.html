UOK UI/ UX SMALL CASE STUDY

Summary

Every day students log in to UOK and answer a series of questions expressing how they feel. The goal of this case study was to improve the daily student wellness experience and enhance engagement. That way students will be wiling more to share their daily experience.

TOOLSTACK

Timeline

3-5 Weeks

Target Audience

Students age 13-18

Group

3 Designers

THE PROBLEM

When searching for cabins, users currently filter through a series of drop downs to find a cabin. It's not obvious for users which months are available and ideally they'd like more flexibility when searching and booking a cabin.

THE HYPOTHESIS

We believe that creating a more optimized search process for users looking to book a cabin will lead to more bookings and overall satisfaction.
