UNPLUGGED UI/ UX SMALL CASE STUDY

Summary

Unplugged allows users to book off-grid cabin experiences, lock away their phone, and find their zen. The goal of the case study was to find solution opportunities and implement these changes to help users find the cabin that was right for them.

TOOLSTACK

Timeline

3-5 Weeks

Target Audience

Nature travellers

Group

2 Designers

THE PROBLEM

When searching for cabins, users currently filter through a series of drop downs to find a cabin. It's not obvious for users which months are available and ideally they'd like more flexibility when searching and booking a cabin.

THE HYPOTHESIS

We believe that creating a more optimized search process for users looking to book a cabin will lead to more bookings and overall satisfaction.

THE SOLUTION

Our team arrived at this solution: creating a more convenient and simpler way for the user to easily book a cabin and view their stay options will make for a more pleasant user experience, generating return users and increasing bookings thereby bringing in more revenue for Unplugged.
