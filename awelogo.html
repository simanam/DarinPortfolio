MAIN LOGO DESIGN

I was asked to create a visual identity for the newly founded design company called Awelogo.

The chosen typeface had to reflect firm itself which is dealing with branding and logo designs. The shape I used for the logo incorporates the first letter of the name which has been generated by two sepeated shapes. The combined shape of the logo represent an arrow that goes upwards. This is to imply that Awelogo offers complete and straightforward solutions, which only benefit the customer’s business and brand identity to higher levels.

As part of the logo colouring, we decided it to have a brighter red so it can be used as an accent on their main website and overall branding as well as red being associated with art.
